About

Hello! I am Joseph Hopkins a Web Development Student. I served 6 years in the United States Marine Corps as a Military Police K9 Handler. After leaving the military I learned that the civilian side did not have as many jobs in my military occupation specialty. To adapt I decided I needed to restart and learn something new. Since then I became a NCCER (National Center for Construction Education and Research) certified welder. I started a side business in welding and woodworking. With a passion for learning I decided to go to STVT (South Texas Vocational Technical Institute) and become an Automotive Service Technician (AST). Upon graduation I began to incorporate my AST certification into my side business. Then Web Development came into the picture. I decided to attend CodeUp and that is what lead me to where I am today.
