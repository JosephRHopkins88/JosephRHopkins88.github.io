About

Hello, my name is Joseph Hopkins and I am a web development student at CodeUp. I served 6 years in the United States Marine Corps as a military police K9 handler. After leaving the military I learned that the civilian side did not have many jobs in my military occupation specialty. To adapt, I decided that I needed to learn something new. Since then, I have become a National Center for Construction Education and Research (NCCER) certified welder and started a business in welding and woodworking. With a passion for learning I decided to go to South Texas Vocational Technical Institute (STVT) and become an Automotive Service Technician (AST). Upon graduation I began to incorporate my AST certification into my welding and woodworking business. Realizing that my desire for lifelong learning was the major driving force since leaving the military I came to the conclusion that I would need to focus on a career that would continually challenge me on an intellectual basis. With that goal in mind, and my enthusiasm for technology in general, I decided that a career in web and software development would allow me to continually grow as a person and excel in a career path that I truly enjoy.
